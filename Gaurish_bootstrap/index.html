Technical Clubs

  • The Society of Coders

    The official computer society club which focuses on doing things rather than learning things.

    The institute's club for the incredibly interesting and growing field of Artificial Intelligence.

  • CipherCell

    CipherCell is an official cybersecurity club of IIIT NR. This club intends to bring general awareness about hacking and to break the stigma surrounding the field.

  • Tech4Gud

    A club that incorporates technological endeavors and social service, whose members strive to make tech that addresses the social, economic and environmental challenges.

IIIT NR MAP

International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur (IIIT-NR), formally Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur, is a state funded institute in Naya Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India. The institute is focused in research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines.

The institute was established by the International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013 of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture of Government of C.G. and NTPC.
